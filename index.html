 What is this?

Rank Up! is a calculator that suggests the best dungeon(s) to run, based on the stamina you have. Plug in your Stamina and/or EXP needed to give it a whirl.
Features:
  • Takes into account Daily Bonus events (such as 1/2 stamina).
  • Customizable settings to only suggest dungeons you have access to.
